## 📘 Project Overview

Developed within the scope of the **Integrative Project (5th Semester - LEI/ISEP)**, this solution is a proof of concept designed for **modern international ports**.

The system aims to modernize port logistics by integrating back-office management with intelligent decision-support systems. It handles complex workflows such as **Vessel Visit Notifications (VVN)**, **Dock Assignment**, and **Cargo Manifest Processing**, while ensuring compliance with international maritime standards (IMO, ISO 6346) and GDPR.

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 🚢 Port Operations Management
* **Vessel & Dock Management:** Registration of vessels, classification constraints, and dynamic management of dock capacity and storage areas.
* **Visit Workflow:** Complete lifecycle management of Vessel Visits (Submission -> Approval -> Auditing).
* **Logistics:** Management of shipping agents, staff qualifications, and physical resource availability.

### 🧠 Intelligent Planning (AI)
* **Dock Assignment Algorithms:** Automatic allocation of vessels to docks based on size, draft, and cargo type.
* **Resource Scheduling:** Optimization of loading/unloading tasks to minimize turnaround time using genetic algorithms/heuristics.

### 🌍 Visualization & Interaction
* **3D Digital Twin:** Interactive visualization of the port infrastructure, allowing users to see vessel positions, dock occupancy, and container stacks in real-time.
* **Dashboarding:** KPIs and statistical analysis for port authority decision-making.
